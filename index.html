About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Miami Herbert Business School, working with Dr. Lan Wang.

Before that, I earned my PhD in Biostatistics from McGill University, supervised by Dr. Erica Moodie and Dr. Sahir Bhatnagar.

Research

My research interests include

  • Statistical Reinforcement Learning

  • Dynamic Treatment Regimens

  • Causal Inference

  • Epidemiology
