About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Miami Herbert Business School under the supervision of Dr. Lan Wang. Before that, I was a PhD student at McGill University supervised by Professors Erica Moodie and Sahir Bhatnagar.

Research

My research interests include

  • Causal Inference
